About Spot

Spot

We are developing a solution for parking problems, because it's a waste of time to find a spot for your car. To resolve this problem we are going to modelate in a scale of two zones in which each one will have 20 spots. We are going to use sensors to detect the toy cars.

We are going to develop an app that will tell you where to park depending on the amount of cars in each zone. There is going to be an administrative page that will tell you real time statistics and analysis.
